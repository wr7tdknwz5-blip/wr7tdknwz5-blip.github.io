Hive Investing

Hive Investing is a personal, single-operator research and publishing system. It analyzes market data and produces written and audio investing content, then automatically publishes that content to the operator's own accounts: blog posts to Blogger, videos to YouTube, and a weekly podcast. It also uses Gmail, Calendar, Drive, and Sheets internally to run that publishing pipeline (notifications, scheduling, working files, and tracking) — all on the operator's own Google account, not on behalf of any other user.
